Demo

Step 1: Enter Information

In our demo, we will take a student, John Doe. John Doe has been facing a mental health crisis due to intense internship stress which has led to mild self-harm.

The first step is to enter our information, then begin to describe the situation. After that, click the send button in the bottom right hand corner to continue.

Step 2: Engage with Bruincare

The next step is to continue the conversation with Bruincare. Respond to questions it may prompt you to understand more about your situation and respond to possible feedback and coping mechanisms provided by Bruincare.

Once you feel that you have shared all your concerns with Bruincare, you are ready for a consultation! Click the blue box at the bottom of the page to continue.

Step 3: Receive Consultation Preparation Notes

Moving on, we receive our consultation preparation notes. It gives us a chat summary of John's interaction with Bruincare as well as a severity score (3).

At the end of the page, click the button at the bottom to arrive at the Bruincare Research Survey.

Step 4: Fill Out the Survey!

The Bruincare Research Survey is designed to give us a better understanding of students' reactions to our product. It allows us to make beneficial changes in future iterations and better understand the role of AI in the mental health space.
