Bruincare

Bruincare is an experimental AI-based product designed to assist CAPS in the consultation and triage process. To begin, share your concerns and/or changes in emotional well-being to prompt conversation with Bruincare. Continue conversation until you have expressed yourself and sufficiently answered prompted questions. Personal information and conversations with Bruincare are kept secure and confidential between only the user and provider.




IF THIS IS A LIFE THREATENING EMERGENCY, CALL 9-1-1 OR GO TO YOUR NEAREST HOSPITAL EMERGENCY ROOM.
